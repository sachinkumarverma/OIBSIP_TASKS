Spiritual Leader

Shri Devkinandan Thakur Ji Maharaj

Age - 44 years

Dharm Ratna

Shri Devkinandan Thakur Ji Maharaj was born on 12th September in the year 1978 in Ohwa Village of Mathura (Birthplace of Lord Krishna) in Uttar Pradesh, India. Born in a Brahmin family, his father Mr. Rajveer Sharma and his mother, both religious and spiritual were believers of Bhagwat Mahapuran. The family was full of joy with his birth as their second child in a family of four sons and two daughters. In childhood he showed signs of his divine insight and greatness. From the age of six he left his home and lived in Shridham Vrindavan where he participated in the famous Rasleela Sansthan of Braj and performed as Lord Krishna and Lord Rama. Since he used to play the role Lord Krishna who is also known as Thakurji, Shri Devkinandan's loved one, his family and his Guruji called him Thakurji. In Shridham Vrindavan he met his spiritual teacher, his Sadhguru, Anant Shri Vibhushit Bhagwat Acharya Purushottam Sharan Shastri Ji. He later received his spiritual initiation under the guru-shishya Parampara as a follower of the Nimbark Sampraday.

Shri Devkinandan Thakurji has attained great spiritual, as well as Vedic knowledge of the divine. When Shri Devkinandan Thakurji was only 13 years old, with the blessings and guidance of his Sadhguru he learned the entire Shrimad Bhagwat Mahapuran. At the age of 18, Shri Devkinandan Thakurji recited and preached the teachings of Shrimad Bhagwat Mahapuran at Shri Ram Mandir, Shahdara in Delhi. The attendees were touched by the divinity in the voice and the simple unique manner in which Shri Devkinandan Thakurji delivered his speeches.

Interesting Facts

  • Shri Devkinandan Thakurji is a spiritual guru as well as a humanist. He is a religious guru, his melodious hymns, discourses bring joy within every soul. A large number of devotees participate in his sankirtan, who put forward the presence of the divine Shri Radha and Lord Shri Krishna.

  • He has Millions of followers across social media platforms including Facebook, Youtube and Instagram.
    More than 4 Million followers on Youtube and 3 Million+ on facebook.

  • Shri Devkinandan Ji popularly known as Thakurji Maharaj is a devotee of Shri Radha Sarveshwarji. He is a great spokesperson of Bhagwat Katha and has made people experience spiritual bliss in many countries around the World.

  • Devkinandan Thakur Ji has been awarded the UP Ratna Award in 2017 for his charitable work. This award was given to him by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath.

  • Titles
    • He is conferred with title of Shantidoot (Messenger of Peace) by Nimbarakacharya Jagadguru Shri Shyamsharan Devacharya for spreading the message of peace and love all around the World.

    • The Shri Brahmin Mahasangh honored his Holiness Shri Devkinandan Thakurji with the rank of 'Acharyindra'.

    • He was conferred with the title of 'Dharm Ratna' in year 2017 by Pujya Jagadguru Shankaracharya.

  • In the year 2001, at the request of his devotees who were extremely impressed by his discourses and verses, they invited him to spread his message to countries outside India and share spiritual insights with people living there.

  • Shri Devkinandan Thakurji accepted the invitation with the permission of his Guru Anant Vibhushit Shri Bhagwatacharya Purushottam Sharan Shastri ji and for the first time gave an opportunity to the people of USA, Canada, Hong Kong, Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, Denmark, Sweden, Norway and Holland from India to be fortunate with his discourses.

  • He is well known Orator of Shrimad Bhagwat katha, Shiv Mahapuran, Garud Puran, Devi Bhagwat Puran, Padma Puran, ShriRam Katha, ShriKrishna Katha, Bhaktmal katha, Brahmavaivarta Puran, Vishnu Puran, Varah Puran and many more.

  • He has done many campaigns to end Dowry system, making Ganga and Yamuna rivers pollution free, Cow protection, Ram Mandir, Water and Environment protection.

  • He established 'Vishwa Shanti Sewa Charitable Trust' in 2006. The trust aims to maintain peace in all the region culturally and economically. It helps poor, disabled and elderly citizens. Aashram activities include preservation of culture, cow sheds, service to orphanchildren, etc. and maintain peace in all communities.

  • Under his leadership, a very beautiful Priyakant Ju temple is built at Shanti Sewa Dham (Vrindavan) which was inaugurated in 2016. This temple is 125 feet tall and very unique in the world because it is built on the structure symbolising lotus flower.
